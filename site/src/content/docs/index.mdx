Welcome to the Omnigram documentation. Omnigram is an AI-native, self-hosted book library and reading service.

**Jellyfin for videos. Immich for photos. Omnigram for books.**

AI is air, not a button — woven into every surface of the reading experience. You never "open AI." You just read, and the experience is transformed.
